The Fortnightly Foodgrain Outlook provides an update on most recent developments in domestic and international foodgrain markets and public foodgrain management operations. Providing an immediate reference for short term planning and decision making, these documents are thus particularly useful for policy-makers, development partners and other food security stakeholders. The Outlook sheds light on changes in domestic prices and imports of rice and wheat, as well as on international price movements and market prospects of the two commodities. It also summarizes the performance of internal foodgrain procurement activities and major distribution programs under the Public Food Distribution System.
