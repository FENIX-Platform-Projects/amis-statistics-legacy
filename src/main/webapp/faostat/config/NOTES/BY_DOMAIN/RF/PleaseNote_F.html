A noter

Continental and regional aggregates only include data for reporting countries. Estimates for non-reporting countries are not included in the continental and regional aggregates. Country notes:
  • Australia: Consumption data 2007 refer to fertilizer year (July/June).
  • Bangladesh: Due to confidentiality of production data since 2008, production figures may be underestimated for N and overestimated for P2O5.
  • Bulgaria: Due to confidentiality of production data since 2008, production figures may be overestimated.
  • Canada: Production and export data refer to fertilizer year (July/June).
  • Colombia: Data refer to 68.8% of the country (22 departments out of 32).
  • Cyprus: Data refer to agricultural activities only in the Government controlled area of the country due to the Turkish invasion of 1974.
  • Czech Republic: Consumption data refer to fertilizer year and exclude self-employed farmers. Due to confidentiality of production data since 2007, production figures may be overestimated for N and underestimated for P2O5.
  • Ethiopia: Consumption from 2004 onwards refers to fertilizer year.
  • France: Data refer to fertilizer year until 2008. Production data from 2008 onwards are not derived from the same surveys as prior years (Current source: the Annual Survey on Production), thus there may be discrepancies among the figures. Due to confidentiality of some data, production may be underestimated.
  • Georgia: Uncontrolled territories are not covered.
  • Germany: Consumption data refer to fertilizer year.
  • India: Data refer to fertilizer year (April/March). Export data for 2007, 2008 and 2009 refer to calendar year.
  • Ireland: Data refer to fertilizer year (October/September).
  • Japan: Data refer to fertilizer year (July/June). Industrial use is included. Non-fertilizer use data from 2006 and production data 2009 are estimated and this may affect consumption data.
  • Kazakhstan: Consumption of P2O5 includes ground phosphate rock.
  • Kyrgyzstan: Consumption data refer to fertilizer year.
  • Latvia: Actual consumption refers to fertilizer year.
  • Mexico: From 2007 the country changed the source for collecting data and this may result in inconsistencies in the time series.
  • Myanmar: Production data refer to fertilizer year.
  • Nepal: Imports by informal and unorganized sectors are not included. Data refer to fertilizer year.
  • Netherlands: Actual consumption refers to fertilizer year.
  • New Zealand: Due to confidentiality of production data since 2007 production figures may be overestimated for N and underestimated for P2O5.
  • Poland: Actual consumption refers to fertilizer year.
  • Russian Federation: Trade data (imports and exports) includes Belarus.
  • Serbia: Not including Kosovo.
  • Serbia and Montenegro: Data refer only to Serbia. Kosovo is not included.
  • Seychelles: Data do not refer to the whole country: only the Seychelles Agricultural Agency Agricultural requisite stores are taken into account.
  • Slovakia: Data on consumption refers to fertilizer year.
  • Tanzania: Data on consumption refers to fertilizer year.
  • Thailand: Due to non availability of production data since 2007, production figures for N may be overestimated.
  • Ukraine: Due to partial confidentiality of production data since 2007, production figures for P2O5 may be underestimated.
  • United Kingdom: Consumption data refer to fertilizer year (July/June). Data include Northern Ireland.
  • USA: Please note that with reference to the N totals, there might be double counting of Ammonia.
