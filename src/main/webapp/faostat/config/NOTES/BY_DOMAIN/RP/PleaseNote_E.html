Please Note

  • ALGERIA: Data refer to sales for use in the agricultural sector expressed in Formulated Products.
  • BURKINA FASO: Starting in 1998, data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • CAMEROON: Up to the year 1992 data refer to quantities of Formulated Products imported into the country.
  • GHANA: Starting in 2002, data are expressed in Formulated products.
  • GUINEA: 2009 data refer to the market area of the town of Conacry. The information was collected from importers and distributors of plant protection products and it relates to the quantity of products available in stock and/or for sales.
  • KENYA: In the years 2000 and 2001 "INSECTICIDES" data do not include "Botanical products and Biologicals" because the percentage of active ingredients is not available for these products.
  • MAURITIUS: Starting in 1994, data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Products.
  • TANZANIA: Data reported are not the actual consumption, as some quantities were imported into the country without importation permits and therefore not recorded.
  • TUNISIA: 2009 data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Products.
  • BAHAMAS: Data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Products. "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES” data include fungicide seed treatments. “OTHER PESTICIDES” data include mineral oils.
  • COSTA RICA: Up to 1997, data are expressed in Formulated Products. Starting in 1998 data are expressed in Active Ingredients.
  • HONDURAS: Data refer to quantities imported into the country for agricultural use. In the years 1990, 1991, 1992 and 1994 data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • JAMAICA: Data refer to total Imports of Formulated Products.
  • MEXICO: Data refer to Imports + Production.
  • NICARAGUA: Data refer to Imports.
  • PANAMA: Data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Product. In the period 2001-2007 "HERBICIDES" data include Plant growth regulators.
  • TRINIDAD AND TOBAGO: Data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • USA: "MINERAL OILS" figures include Sulfur Oil and Other Pesticides; "HERBICIDES" figures include Plant growth regulators. "RODENTICIDES" figures include Molluscicides, aquatic and fish/bird pesticides, and other miscellaneous conventional pesticides.
  • BOLIVIA: In the period 1990-1993 data are expressed in Formulated Products. In the period 2000-2007 data do not cover the entire country and they refer to non-toxic pesticides only.
  • CHILE: Data refer to Imports in Formulated Produdcts for use in Agriculture, Forestry and Veterinary and Sanitary sectors. Whereas, the data for the years 1999 and 2004 refer to sales and are expressed in Active Ingredients.
  • COLOMBIA: Starting in 2001, data refer to Production+Imports-Export. In the period 1990 to 1995, data referring to "MINERAL OILS" include the adjuvants.
  • ECUADOR: Data refer to Imports.
  • PERU: Data refer to Imports. Data on "SEED TREATMENT FUNGICIDES" refer to disinfectants.
  • SURINAME: Starting in 2002, data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Products.
  • BANGLADESH: Data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • BHUTAN: Data refer to total Imports of Formulated Products.
  • CYPRUS: Starting in 2000, data refer to Imports expressed in Formulated Products; In the period 2000-2007, data on "OTHER HERBICIDES" refer to Urea derivates, Sulfonyl Ureas and Uracil.
  • IRAN: Starting in 2000, data are expressed in Formulated Products; "OTHER PESTICIDES" data include Granules & Emulsion Oil.
  • ISRAEL: "INSECTICIDES" figures include Rodenticides.
  • JAPAN: Data refer to sales for use in the agricultural sector including agricultural chemicals for turf and forest. Data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES". Data on biologicals are not included, because most of the active ingredients are expressed in units other than metric tons (e.g. the number of natural enemy in the products and Colony Forming Unit (CFU) for microbial pesticides). "OTHER INSECTICIDES" data include the Mineral oils.
  • JORDAN: In the period 2001-2007 data are expressed in Formulated Products. Data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES".
  • KOREA REPUBLIC OF: Data refer to delivery of Agricultural Chemical from the manufacturers. Starting in 2001, data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES".
  • KYRGYZSTAN: Data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES” and “FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES".
  • LAOS: Data refer to Imports through the Department of Agriculture and the Provincial Offices of Agriculture and Forestry.
  • MALAYSIA: Data refer to Imports.
  • OCCUPIED PALESTINIAN TERRITORY: Data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • SYRIA: Data are expressed in Formulated Products.
  • AUSTRIA: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. "INSECTICIDES" data include insecticide seed treatments, acaricides, molluscicides, nematicides and synergists. "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES" data include fungicide seed treatments. “OTHER FUNGICIDES” data refer to the amount of Sulfur and Copper products..
  • BELGIUM: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. "HERBICIDES" figures also include quantity of herbicides sold for non-agricultural use.
  • DENMARK: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture.
  • ESTONIA: Data from 1992 to 1996 refer to use of pesticides in the enterprises only; starting in 1997 data refer to use of pesticides in both enterprises and private farms. Starting in 2002, "UREA DERIVATES" data also include Sulfunyl Ureas; "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES" data include Seed treatment fungicides.
  • FINLAND: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. Data on seed treatments Insecticides and Fungicides are included in the groups "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES" respectively.
  • FRANCE: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. "OTHER PESTICIDES" data refer to Mineral oils, Nematicides and other products that have not been elsewhere classified.
  • GERMANY: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. "OTHER PESTICIDES" data refer to "Inert Gases for storage protections" which is a specific category for Germany not included in the insecticides sum.
  • GREECE: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture.
  • IRELAND: Data refer to quantities imported into the country for agricultural use. Data referring to the herbicides products "AMIDES" and "DINITROANILINES" are included in "OTHER HERBICIDES" in the years 1996 and 1997.
  • ITALY: Up to 1995 data refer to distribution of Formulated Products for use in the agricultural sector. From 1996 data refer to distribution of Active Ingredients.
  • LATVIA: Data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES"
  • LITHUANIA: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. Starting in 2002, data on seed treatments are not reported separately, but included in each of the group "INSECTICIDES" and "FUNGICIDES & BACTERICIDES".
  • MACEDONIA: Data cover Agricultural enterprises and cooperatives but exclude individual agricultural holdings.
  • NORWAY: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture also including plant protection products used outside traditional agriculture, e.g.: private gardens etc.
  • POLAND: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture.
  • PORTUGAL: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture. The increase in sales between 1994 and 1995 is mainly due to an increase in the number of companies included in the survey.
  • SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO: Data refer to formulated products. Starting in 1999, data for Kosovo and Metohia are not included.
  • SLOVAKIA: Data on “MINERAL OILS” include only herbicide mineral oils; whereas insecticide mineral oils are covered under “OTHER INSECTICIDES”.
  • SLOVENIA: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in agriculture.
  • SPAIN: Up to 1995 data were collected as volume of Formulated Products. Estimated volumes of Active Ingredients are derived from data using a correction factor which is calculated for each year based on average products usage. ECPA source.
  • SWITZERLAND: Starting in 2007, the country improved the data collection methodology as to include data gathered from all pesticides suppliers.
  • UK: Data refer to sales for application to crops and seeds in the agricultural sector.
  • FIJI: Data refer to formulated products.
  • FRENCH POLYNESIA: Data refer to imports as registered on the entry to the country. The registration is under the control of the officers of the Plant Protection Department.
  • NEW CALEDONIA: Data refer to imports for agricultural use.
