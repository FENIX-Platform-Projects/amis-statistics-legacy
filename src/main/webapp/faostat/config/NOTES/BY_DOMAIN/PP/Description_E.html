The Prices domain covers data from 1991 onwards for over 130 countries world-wide, representing 97% of the world’s value of production at 1999-2001 International Dollar Prices.

Price Links:
  • International commodity prices, database maintained by FAO’s Trade and Markets Division
  • http://www.fao.org/giews/pricetool/, national basic food prices database maintained by FAO's Trade and Markets Division
  • Agricultural prices and price indices, time series on selected commodities in EU member-States, Eurostat
  • http://laborsta.ilo.org/, statistics on retail prices disseminated by the International Labour Organization (ILO)
  • Monthly Bulletin of Statistics, from the UN Statistics Division.
  • Commodity Price Data (Pink Sheet), Monthly commodity price data in summary format, the World Bank
