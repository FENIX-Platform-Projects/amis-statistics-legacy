The wholesale price of rice decreased by 1.4% to
18.6 Tk/kg over the fortnight ending August 6th.
The retail price of rice inDhaka Cityremained at
its end of June level of 21.0 Tk/kg.<br><br>
<li>As a result, the retail margin increased by 12.5%
up to 2.38 Tk/Kg.</li>
<li>There were no variations in the wholesale and
retail prices of atta compared to the previous
fortnight.</li>
